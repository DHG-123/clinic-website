Our Specialized Services

We provide comprehensive care across two major departments, ensuring advanced treatments for all age groups.

Orthopaedic Care

Joint Replacement

Total Hip and Knee replacements.

We use minimally invasive techniques for faster recovery and reduced post-op pain, ensuring you get back on your feet sooner.

Fracture & Trauma

Expert care for traumatic injuries.

Specialized treatment for complex fractures and bone infections using the latest fixation methods led by Dr. Naveen Kempe Gowda.

Sports Injuries

Ligament and muscle care.

Diagnosis and treatment of ACL/PCL tears and meniscus injuries for athletes and active individuals.

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Antenatal Care

Standard and high-risk pregnancy care.

Regular check-ups, growth monitoring, and nutritional guidance to ensure a safe delivery for mother and child.

Gynaecology

Women's health and wellness.

Treatment for PCOD, menstrual irregularities, and minimally invasive gynecological surgeries.

Health Diagnostics

In-house lab tests.

Accurate and timely diagnostic tests for hormone levels, blood work, and comprehensive health checkups.

A unit of Sapthashwa healthcare and diagnostics.
